The edges of the nectosphere graph: a link between nodes or within a node.

==== Typical bases for a link: ====

* common places (city, house, room);
* common events (war, accident, celebration);
* common emotions (fear, guilt, delight).

[[Nectospheric traffic]] spreads along these links.
