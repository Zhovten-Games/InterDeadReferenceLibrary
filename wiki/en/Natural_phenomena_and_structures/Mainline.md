A high-load “trunk” in the [[nectosphere]]: a chain of nodes and links along which a constant large volume of traffic flows (memories, states, recurring scenarios).

==== Features: ====

* forms in places of historical/emotional super-density;
* defines the main direction of movement of “flows of the dead”;
* <u>breakthroughs into the world of the living</u> are possible at mainline intersections.
