The minimal unit of “personality” in the [[nectosphere]].

It is a stable set of patterns: memories, habits, fears, emotional reactions.

==== Features: ====

* can be more or less coherent (from an almost whole personality to separate fragments);
* in the case of strong degradation, the node turns into a set of automatic reactions and separate flashes.
