A section of the [[nectosphere]] where several mainlines intersect.

==== Properties: ====

* a zone of increased activity;
* when it coincides with a physical place (house, district, station), it creates long-lived “cursed places”;
* a living medium who ends up here can become a <u>carrier of mass traffic</u> (a written “book” for other people’s stories).
