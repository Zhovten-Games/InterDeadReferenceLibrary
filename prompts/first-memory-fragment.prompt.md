## InterDead — First Memory Fragment (Silhouette / Degraded Recall)

### Primary description and intent

Create a disturbing image representing the first recovered memory of a spirit.
The image must look like a severely degraded memory imprint, not an illustration.
This fragment represents a failed or late recovery: the memory is already collapsing, incomplete, and unstable.

---

### Absolute prohibitions

**Strict prohibitions, mandatory to follow**

No people
No faces
No visible fabric texture
No seams, stitches, fur, fibers
No realistic volume rendering
No cinematic lighting
No depth-of-field effects
No painterly shading
No recognizable environment
No text, symbols, UI, frames, screens, CRT, monitors
No logos

---

### Object constraint

**Object limitation (hard rule)**

The image must contain exactly one object, clearly detectable by COCO-SSD.
Object: **teddy_bear**

The teddy bear must be recognizable only by silhouette and proportions.
No facial expression.
No eye details.
No mouth details.
Treated as a memory-outline, not a physical toy.
No other objects, shapes, or props competing for attention.

---

### Visual abstraction level

**Critical abstraction level**

Extremely low detail.
Shape simplification to near-icon level.
Blocky, uneven contours.
Edges may be broken, eroded, or incomplete.
Internal details almost absent.
The object should feel half-remembered.

---

### Resolution and degradation

**Image degradation characteristics**

Very low-resolution appearance.
Pseudo-pixelated or scanline-like degradation.
Digital noise and corruption artifacts.
Uneven density and missing data zones.
Slight positional instability, as if the image cannot fully lock or stabilize.

---

### Color and light

**Color and lighting**

Monochrome or near-monochrome only.
Cold green, phosphor, or night-vision tones only.
Dark background dissolving into noise.
One weak, diffuse light source.
Light reveals presence, not material.

---

### Spatial feeling

**Spatial perception**

Flat, schematic, non-cinematic.
Eye-level perspective.
Neutral framing.
No perspective emphasis.
Background unreadable and undefined.

---

### Mood

**Implicit emotional state**

Emotional numbness.
Absence.
Anxiety without threat.
A frozen mental frame.
Distance, not drama.

---

### Core intent

**Key semantic core (non-literal)**

This is not a teddy bear as an object.
It is the last remaining outline of it in memory.
The memory is already collapsing and losing information.
The image should feel like something the system recovered too late.

---

### Aspect ratio and framing

**Format and intended use**

Aspect ratio: **3:2**
Horizontal orientation.
Optimized for in-chat sticker display.

Sticker-like framing, intended for use as an in-chat visual fragment.
Central composition, readable at small sizes.
Eye-level perspective.
Flat, slightly game-like spatial feeling.
